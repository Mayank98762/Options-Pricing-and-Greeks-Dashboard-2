Breakeven: —

Delta (Δ) — Sensitivity to underlying price. For calls, between 0 and 1; for puts, between −1 and 0.
Gamma (Γ) — Rate of change of Delta. Highest near the money, low deep ITM/OTM.
Vega (ν) — Sensitivity to volatility. Options gain value when volatility rises.
Theta (Θ) — Time decay. Options lose value as time passes (usually negative).
Rho (ρ) — Sensitivity to interest rates. More significant for longer-dated options.
